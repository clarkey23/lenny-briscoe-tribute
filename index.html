'I'll be in mourning for the next five minutes'

The man was not perfect. The small insights we were provided about his personal life revealed many problems. Broken home. Poor relationships with childen. However, we meet a man who had come to terms with all of his earlier miscomings and found purpose as a detective.

We meet a smart, witty man who has built rapport with many characters throughout NY and was great at his job. He cut through the BS and was a pleasure to watch on TV.
